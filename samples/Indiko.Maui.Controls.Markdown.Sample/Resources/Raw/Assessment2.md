## Now let’s check your focus
::spacer 32

The focus scale scores from 0 to 20 and contains 3 thresholds:

**Sharp and consistent**
You’re thinking clearly and staying focused. Your concentration is in a healthy range.

::spacer 32
![](https://directus-926401349027.europe-west2.run.app/assets/c033d14d-d032-4f6c-8711-dbf33b7105e5.svg){ width=65 height=20 }
::spacer 4

**Occasional lapses**
You may notice your focus slips at times. Small changes to your routine could help sharpen attention.

::spacer 32
![](https://directus-926401349027.europe-west2.run.app/assets/6e0c4653-55f1-4984-a8b8-7f900c8c967d.svg){ width=65 height=20 }
::spacer 4

**Easily distracted**
Your focus is lower than usual. If this keeps happening, try steps to reduce distractions and restore mental energy..


::spacer 32
> ### WHY THIS MATTERS
>
> Focus helps you perform at your best — whether at work, learning, or daily tasks. Tracking your score shows how your routines and device sessions may be helping you stay sharp.
::spacer 32
